Izak (He/Him)

Posted on 3/19/2024

Izak is a 1st-year student at 3805, joining after a promotional for 3805 was posted, and his fascination for robotics grew. Shortly after joining, Izak found a group of team members who were building and flying cardboard RC planes outside of class. He gladly joined and further developed his skills as a builder and as a team member.

Izak's biggest achievement was his involvement in improving the competition robot's intake mechanism as well as building and testing the paper airplane launcher for 3805. Alongside building, Izak has also assisted with some small coding endeavors.
