Our Members

Evan

Evan is a fourth-year robotics student with 3805. Starting in 2019, they were invited to join a year early by the teacher at the time, who noticed their curiosity and fascination with engineering. Evan started out as a member of the build team and has continued since, even acting as the build mentor in 9880. He remains part of the build team and helps the Business and Outreach team with graphic design and fundraiser campaigns.

Izak

Izak is a 1st-year student at 3805, joining after a promotional for 3805 was posted, and his fascination for robotics grew. Shortly after joining, Izak found a group of team members who were building and flying cardboard RC planes outside of class. He gladly joined and further developed his skills as a builder and as a team member.

Jacen

Jacen is a second-year student with 3805. He found the class during enrollment and joined because it sounded fun. His biggest accomplishment has been working on the main competition robot. His favorite thing about 3805 is the members of the team and building robots. He is excited to continue building next season.
